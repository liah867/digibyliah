About Us

digicam by liah is a small, second-hand digital camera store located in the town of Brookemill.
The shop specializes in affordable pre-owned DSLR and mirrorless cameras, lenses, and accessories, carefully
tested to ensure quality and reliability. Owned by a local photography enthusiast, digicam by liah offers friendly customer service and helpful advice for
beginners, students, and hobbyists looking to start or upgrade their photography journey without spending too much.
